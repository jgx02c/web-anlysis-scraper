The use of mini rebounders is becoming increasingly popular. bellicon not only offers the perfect equipment for fitness studios but also supports companies, hospitals, physiotherapy practices, rehab centers, sports clubs, and various other institutions in finding the best solution for their unique needs. Innovative Equipment for Fitness Studios, Companies, and Institutions. bellicon provides comprehensive, customized solutions for companies, rehab centers, sports clubs, and other institutions looking to integrate both intense jumping fitness and gentle health training into their programs. Options include both the robust, larger bellicon Jumping models and the classic round mini-rebounders – tailored to meet each organization’s specific requirements. Jumping Fitness is a dynamic full-body workout conducted on specially designed bellicon mini-rebounders. It provides an intense yet joint-friendly workout that enhances muscle strength, endurance, and coordination. Particularly popular in Germany, Jumping Fitness has over 50,000 active participants per week. Classes are accompanied by high-energy music, engaging both body and mind. Suitable for both beginners and advanced athletes, the training can be adapted to individual fitness levels. Subscription Packages: bellicon offers flexible subscription models, including monthly payments and rental options. The bellicon certification provides you with the know-how to train both beginners and advanced participants safely and effectively on the mini rebounder. Exclusive Benefits for Instructors and Studios: As a certified trainer, you can join the bellicon Academy community and benefit from numerous perks. Membership gives you access to exclusive music tracks and choreographies specially developed for Jumping Fitness. You’ll also receive discounts on advanced training and exclusive offers from bellicon+, bellicon’s fitness streaming service. There are special flex subscription options that include the use of a bellicon mini rebounder. You can also join a Facebook community with over 1,500 trainers from Germany and Switzerland to share experiences and ideas. A special highlight is the GEMA-free playlists, curated by an in-house DJ, perfectly tailored to Jumping Fitness training with an hour of music between 118 and 135 BPM. Courses and Certifications: bellicon Academy provides various training programs that certify you as a bellicon trainer. You can choose between the Jumping Fitness training, focused on dynamic and intense training, and the Health training, emphasizing health-focused training for everyone. Both options are available either online or in-person, allowing you to learn flexibly according to your needs. During and after the online basic training, you have the option to rent a bellicon mini rebounder for three months to use in your own practice and to try out the training segments.
