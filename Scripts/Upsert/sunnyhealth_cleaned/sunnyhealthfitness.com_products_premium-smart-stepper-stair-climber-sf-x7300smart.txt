It features an Advanced Electromagnetic system (EMS) with 25 resistance levels and 36 pre-programmed workouts, offering a customizable and high-intensity training experience. The integrated pulse sensors and LED digital monitor track time, steps, height, calories, heart rate, and steps per minute, ensuring real-time progress monitoring for an optimized workout. For enhanced safety, the climber includes infrared auto-stop sensors, full-length handrails, and a sturdy docking step. Built for durability, it supports up to 400 lbs and is designed to withstand intense, long-term use. Connect via Bluetooth to the FREE SunnyFit app, gaining access to thousands of workouts, virtual routes, and personalized training sessions. Additional convenience features include a bottle holder, device holder, and wireless charger for an uninterrupted workout experience.
